I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), supervised by Prof. Di Huang. Before that, Before that I received my master degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), China, in Jan. 2023 and B.E degree from the College of Inteligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU), China, in Jul. 2020. I interned at Alibaba DAMO Academy.

My research interests focus on Generative AI (GenAI), Generative Adversarial Networks and Diffusion models.
